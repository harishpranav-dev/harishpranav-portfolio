02 — Flagship case study

CareerCraftAI

careercraftai.in ↗

The problem

India adds millions of freshers to the job market every year, and most get the same three pieces of advice: do certifications, fix your LinkedIn, keep applying. Generic guidance at national scale. Meanwhile employers drown in identical résumés. Both sides lose.

The build

An AI-powered career platform that treats a fresher as a person, not a keyword — guidance, preparation and matching for students, freshers and employers. Next.js and React up front, Node and Express behind it, PostgreSQL through Prisma, Claude and GPT doing the reasoning.

Where it stands

Live in production at careercraftai.in, serving all three sides of the market. The numbers change weekly — mail me for the current ones. I'd rather tell you the truth on the day you ask than print a stale stat here.

"The hard part wasn't the AI. It was making AI output feel trustworthy to a 21-year-old betting their first job on it."

04 — How I actually work

Three rules I keep re-learning on purpose.

Problem first, stack second.

I don't pick a technology and hunt for uses. I find the thing people tolerate because "that's just how it is" — then build the fix.

AI-native, not AI-dependent.

Claude, GPT and Gemini sit inside both my workflow and my products. The judgment about what actually ships stays mine.

Ship, then sharpen.

One real user on version one teaches more than a month of polishing version zero. Production is the honest reviewer.
